As a farmer, we believe that it is crucial that you can easily organize your inventory and post new inventory. So we've provided you will several links below to do just that.

Post

This is the page where you post new goods. All the information you need to post is located here. (The link is also located in the upper right-hand side of every page).

Dashboard

Two very important tools are located on this page: Inventory and Orders. So we will cover them separately.

Inventory

The Inventory tab on this page contains a full list of all your inventory and information regarding your invertory, including quantity remaining. You can also update goods from this page by click the update button at the right side of a given row. Most importantly, the update page where you can update quantity remaining so that you do not have to re-post the same good. You will also receive an email when you have low stock of a certain good.

Orders

The Orders tab is a list of all the orders you have sold, delivered and undelivered. From here you have the option to "close out" orders by marking them as delivered, assuming they have been delivered. But doing this is merely for your own records.

PS: you can access this page by hovering over your username in the upper right-hand corner of the screen and selecting "Dashboard" from the dropdown menu.

Cart

As a Farmer, you are still able to purchase goods from other farmers. If you choose to do so, all goods that you have added to your cart will be here and this is where you place your order.
